Silk Route Spa Resort

Silk Route Spa Resort, located on the northern riverbank of the Brahmaputra in Gondmow village near Sualkuchi, offers a serene escape just 30 km from Guwahati and the airport. Surrounded by natural beauty, the resort features stunning views of hills, lakes, and forests. Amenities include a restaurant, parking, room service, laundry, and safety features like CCTV and fire extinguishers. Guests enjoy a tranquil stay with migratory birdwatching from October to April.
