Lake Side Resort

At Lake Side Resort Neermahal in Sonamura, you'll be 8 minutes by foot from Neermahal. This hotel is 14.3 mi (23 km) from Mainimati Museum and 14.3 mi (23 km) from Salban Vihara. Enjoy a meal at Restaurant, or stay in and take advantage of the hotel's room service (during limited hours). Free self parking is available onsite. Make yourself at home in one of the 55 guestrooms. Cable television is provided for your entertainment. Bathrooms have showers and complimentary toiletries.

Woodland Park Hotel

6 km from Agartala Railway Station, 1 km from Ujjayanta Palace, In-house restaurant Located in the heart of the city, Hotel Woodland Park features comfortable and well-decked rooms equipped with air-conditioner, cable television, intercom, Wi-Fi, mineral water (chargeable), study table and attached bathrooms. The hotel is in close proximity to Astabal Bus Stop (1.3 km), ML Plaza (2 km), Tripura High Court (4 km) and Agartala City Center (2 km) and offers facilities such as conference hall, complimentary Wi-Fi, room service , central AC and parking facility. Services such as doctor on call, laundry, taxi service, transfers and sightseeing can be availed at an extra cost. In-house restaurant serves delicious and tasty Bengali, Chinese, Indian and Continental delicacies. Hotel Woodland Park is ideal for both leisure and business travellers.
