The Greenwood Tezpur

Offering a spa and wellness centre and a fitness centre, The Greenwood Hotel is located in Tezpur. The picturesque park of Chitralekha Udyan is 6 km from the property. Free WiFi access is available. Each modern air-conditioned room here will luggage storage. The property offers free parking. The Mahabhairav Temple is 7 km, the Nameri National Park is 35 km and the Kaziranga National Park is 45 km. The Salonibari Tezpur Airport and the Dekargaon Railway Station are within 3 km while the Tezpur ASTC Bus Station is 5 km. The Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport is 165 km. Flavors serve multi-cuisine while Gardenia is a 24-hour coffee shop. Private dining is possible with 24-hour room service.

Cygnett Inn Imperial Tezpur

Boasting a bar, Cygnett Inn Imperial Tezpur is situated in Tezpur. Featuring a fitness centre, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. There is a restaurant serving Chinese cuisine, and free private parking is available. The rooms at the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV and a safety deposit box. At Cygnett Inn Imperial Tezpur rooms come with bed linen and towels. Breakfast is available each morning, and includes buffet, à la carte and continental options. Staff at the accommodation are always available to provide advice at the reception. The nearest airport is Tezpur Airport, 9 km from Cygnett Inn Imperial Tezpur. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.3 for a two-person trip.
