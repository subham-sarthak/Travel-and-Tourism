Vantawng Falls

Best Time To Visit:- between September and January.

It is the highest waterfall in Mizoram. Vantawng Falls is the highest and most spectacular of the waterfalls and cascades in the fast-flowing rivers of Mizoram. The height of the fall is recorded as 750 feet (230 m) Though it is difficult to get close to it because of the sheer forested hillsides surrounding it, a viewing tower has been constructed.

Locattion :- located 5 km south of Thenzwal in Serchhio district. located on the bank of vanva river.

Dampa Tiger Reserve & Sanctuary

Best Time To Visit:- between November and march.

Dampa Tiger Reserve or Dampha Tiger Reserve is a tiger reserve of western Mizoram, India. It was declared a tiger reserve in 1994 and is part of Project Tiger.The tropical forests of Dampa Tiger Reserve are home to a diverse flora and fauna. It consists of forest interpolated with steep precipitous hills, deep valleys, jungle streams, ripping rivulets, natural salts licks.

Homestay facilities are available

Location :- it is located in the Lushai hills in Mizoram.

Mizoram State Museum

Best Time To Visit:- anytime in a day between 1.00pm to 4.00pm.

The museum was established in April 1977 by the Tribal Research Institute under Education Department. From 1989, it came under Art and Culture department.It is an ethnographic museum with multipurpose collections on display. There are five galleries: Textile Gallery, Ethnology, History, Anthropology, Natural History, and an Archaeology Terrace. The collection occupies four floors.

Homestay facilities are available

Location :- located in Aizawl, Mizoram

Solomon's Temple

Best Time To Visit :- During december

The temple is one of the largest in Mizoram state. The foundation stone was laid on December 23, 1996, and construction finished 20 years later, with a Christmas service in 2017 inaugurating the temple. The interior of the Temple building is also square, measuring 120 ft. on each side. The Temple has 32 windows, 32 ventilations, and 32 skylights. It is a multi-story building.

Location :- On the western outskirts of Aizawl.

Phawngpui National Park

Best Time To Visit:- Located in the Lawngtlai district towards southeast of Mizoram.

Phawngpui Blue Mountain National Park is one of the two national parks of India in Mizoram, the other and the larger being Murlen National Park. It bears the name of the mountain Phawngpui, often called the Blue Mountain of Mizoram, which is the highest mountain peak in the state. The national park covers the entire mountain along with the surrounding reserve forest.

Homestay facilities are available

Location :- Located in the Lawngtlai district towards southeast of Mizoram.

Khawnglung Wildlife Sanctuary

Best Time To Visit :- between October and march

Khawnglung is a mountain situated near Pangzawl in Aizawl – Lunglei road about 160 km from Aizawl. With sheer cliffs on all sides the mountain has always been a natural shelter for man and animal for ages. Declared a wildlife sanctuary in 2000, Khawnglung covers an area of 35 sq.km and is rich in flora and fauna, particularly primates and birds. It however requires trekking for about 10 km to reach the sanctuary.

Homestay facilities are available

Location :- 140 km away from Aizawl
