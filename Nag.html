Japfu Peak

Best Time To Visit :- during the summer months. In Summer, between March to May when the weather is pleasant and the trails are dry.

Japfu Peak Trek in Nagaland is a popular trekking destination in the northeastern region of India. It is a moderate to difficult trek with an altitude of 3048 meters above sea level. The trek offers scenic views of the lush green forests, flora and fauna, and the famous Dzukou Valley.

Location :- located in japfill district, kohima Nagaland.

Ntanki national park

Best Time To Visit :- Winters are the best time to visit and the months of November to February

It was first designated as a national park in 1993. Among the species that inhabit the park are the rare hoolock gibbon, golden langur, hornbill, Asian palm civet, black stork, tiger, white-breasted kingfisher, monitor lizard, python and sloth bear. The name "Ntangki" is derived from the Zeme dialect of the Zeliangrong Nagas.

Homestay facilities are available

Location :- located in Peren District of Nagaland

Kacharii Ruins

Best Time To Visit:- during winter time.

The Kachari Rajbari Ruins are a series of mushroom domed pillars. They were created by the Kachari Kingdom, which ruled Dimapur before the Ahom invasion.Their origin and purpose are largely mysterious. Some pillars still stand in all their glory but others have crumbled down. It is believed that a game similar to chess was played there with the mushroom domes.

Location :- located in Dimapur, Nagaland, in Northeast India

Dzudza Retreat

Best Time To Visit:- between April to August

Dzukou Valley is one of the most beautiful places in Nagaland. This valley is well known for its natural environment and seasonal flowers. Enjoy the solitude of the place and connect with your natural surroundings with this easy trek. Though Dzukou Valley is millions of years old and absolutely desolate, this trek will take you across emerald green hills and pristine valleys. The valley is home to amazing varieties of lilies and rhododendrons, apart from several bird species

Location :- 30km from kohima

Mon Village

Best Time To Visit:- between October to may

Mon District is the home of the Konyak Nagas. The Konyaks are famous for their tattooed faces and bodies. The most colourful festival of the Konyaks, Aoling is observed during the first week of April every year. Konyaks are the largest ethnic group among the Nagas.Memorial stones are also found in front of the Angh's palace.There is a wooden monument measuring 8 feet in height and 12 feet in breadth – believed to be constructed by heavenly angels

Location :- It is situated in the northern Naga Hills, about 10 miles (16 km) northwest of the Myanmar (Burma) border
